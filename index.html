How it works

Three things. That's it.

No product recommendations. No hair typing. Start logging on day one and let your pattern emerge naturally.

1

Log your care

Wash days. Rest days. Light care. Keep it honest, not perfect. Even what didn't happen still tells you something.

2

See your rhythm

Your pattern forms visually as a quiet record of what you're actually doing, not what you planned to do.

3

Receive reflections

When something meaningful shifts, Rooted offers a reflection. Not constant updates. Only when there's something worth noticing.

Pattern reflections

Insight, not analytics.

Rooted moves at your pace, not on a timer. It waits until something real starts to take shape, then offers you a reflection that actually feels worth reading.

  • A primary observation about your rhythm. Written in plain language, not metrics.
  • A connection to your goal - how your pattern relates to what you're working towards.
  • A calm reflection question, not a prompt to act. A prompt to notice.
  • A permanent archive. Each reflection is frozen at the moment it's written. Something to return to.
